Music Activities and Achievements

Music is a passion that helps develop creativity, discipline, and complex pattern recognition.

Carnatic Flute Performance

I am trained in playing the Carnatic Flute (Venu), which is central to South Indian classical music. This requires a deep understanding of complex Ragas (melodic frameworks) and Taalas (rhythmic cycles). The precision and technique needed for this instrument directly strengthen my focus and detail-orientation, similar to the demands of engineering.

  • Style: Dedicated training in Carnatic (South Indian Classical) music.
  • Skills: Improvisation (Manodharma) within Raga structures, mastery of complicated fingerings and breath control.
  • Performances: Participate in local cultural events and school assemblies.

Saxophone Family Mastery

I have developed proficiency across the saxophone family, starting with the Alto Saxophone and currently advancing my skills through the Tenor and eventually maturing to the Baritone Saxophone. Playing multiple instruments in this family requires adaptive embouchure and transposing knowledge, applying a systematic approach to technical execution.

  • Instruments: Alto Saxophone (primary), Tenor Saxophone (intermediate), Baritone Saxophone (target).
  • Ensembles: Member of the school band, focusing on classical and jazz repertoire.
  • Skills: Transposition, ensemble collaboration, advanced breath support, and dynamic control.
