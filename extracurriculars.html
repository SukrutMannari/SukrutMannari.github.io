Other Extracurricular Activities

Developing discipline, teamwork, and competitive engineering skills through diverse activities.

FIRST LEGO League (FLL) Competition

Participating in FLL is a direct application of my passion for engineering and robotics. Our team is responsible for designing, building, and programming an autonomous LEGO Mindstorms robot to complete complex missions on a themed challenge mat. This environment teaches collaborative problem-solving, rapid prototyping, and software logic under pressure.

  • Role: Programmer and Mechanical Designer.
  • Focus Areas: Mission strategy, iterative robot design, and programming using graphical or text-based language (e.g., EV3-G or Python).
  • Skills: Teamwork, competitive project management, and rapid implementation of robotic solutions.

Taekwondo

Taekwondo provides rigorous training in physical discipline, self-control, and perseverance. The structured environment emphasizes goal setting (achieving belt ranks) and focused execution of complex forms (Poomsae) and sparring techniques. This activity builds confidence and the mental fortitude required to tackle difficult engineering challenges.

  • Focus: Poomsae (forms), breaking techniques, and developing concentration.
  • Skills: Discipline, physical fitness, mental toughness, and goal achievement.

Computer Club (with Mr. Hahn)

The Computer Club is where I explore advanced coding concepts and software development beyond the classroom curriculum under the guidance of Mr. Hahn. We work on various projects, ranging from web development basics to algorithmic problem-solving, which is essential for understanding the logic that drives autonomous and remote systems.

  • Mentor: Mr. Hahn.
  • Focus: Python programming, website structure (HTML/CSS), and algorithmic thinking.
  • Skills: Coding fundamentals, logical structure, and practical application of computer science.
